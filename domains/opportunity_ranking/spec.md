# Opportunity Ranking Domain Spec

- Decision question: Which opportunity option has the highest risk-adjusted value under current constraints?
- Unit of evaluation: one opportunity option in one context
- Success outcome: ranked opportunity score with manual adjudication rules
- Time horizon: 3-6 months
- Evidence sources: structural, comparative, constraint, behavioral, risk
- Constraints: capital, team bandwidth, execution complexity, time to payoff
- Benchmark rule: compare options by composite score with uncertainty and risk penalties
