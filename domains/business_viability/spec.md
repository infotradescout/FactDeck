# Business Viability Domain Spec

- Decision question: Is this concept viable in this geography under current constraints?
- Unit of evaluation: one concept in one geography
- Success outcome: revenue viability / survival / margin viability
- Time horizon: 6-12 months
- Evidence sources: structural, comparative, constraint, behavioral, risk
- Constraints: capital, skills, geography, seasonality, competition
- Benchmark rule: compare options by final score with uncertainty and risk adjustments
